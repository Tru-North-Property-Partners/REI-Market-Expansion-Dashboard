Lead Backlog: markets we have cost data for but are not tracking yet

Market Opportunity Score (0-100)
Sweet-spot model: Days-to-Pending 25% (ideal 15-35d) · Months of Inventory 25% (ideal 1.5-3.5mo) · Cash Sales % 20% (ideal 15-35%) · Sale/List Velocity 15% · Population Growth 15%. Too-hot and too-cold markets are both penalized.
Ad Market Score (0-100)
Where to spend ad dollars: Opportunity 40% · Metro Size 20% · Our Lead Flow 20% · Price-Drop Motivation 10% · Affordability 10%.
ROI Score (0-100)
Rewards strong deal fundamentals in markets where competition is low. It is built mainly from the Opportunity score, with a bonus for low competition, and a penalty for markets too small to reliably source deals, agents or cash buyers. Lead cost is deliberately left out for now - our cost figures are still being verified, so they are shown for reference only and do not move the score.
Hidden Gem Flag
A market is flagged a Hidden Gem when it is mid-sized (roughly 30k to 150k), low competition (≤3 of 10), and still scores 55+ on opportunity, while being big enough to actually find sellers, agents and cash buyers. Often overlooked by larger buyers.
Novation / MLS
DOM < 20, MOI < 2, Sale/List > 100% - fast, tight, full-price markets.
Wholesale
DOM 20-45 with Cash % > 18% - strong investor buyer pool.
Wholetail / Creative / Hold
Slower or higher-inventory markets favor wholetail, sub-2 / creative terms, or buy-and-hold appreciation plays.
