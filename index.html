Market Opportunity Score (0-100)
Sweet-spot model: Days-to-Pending 25% (ideal 15-35d) · Months of Inventory 25% (ideal 1.5-3.5mo) · Cash Sales % 20% (ideal 15-35%) · Sale/List Velocity 15% · Population Growth 15%. Too-hot and too-cold markets are both penalized.
Ad Market Score (0-100)
Where to spend ad dollars: Opportunity 40% · Metro Size 20% · Our Lead Flow 20% · Price-Drop Motivation 10% · Affordability 10%.
ROI Score (0-100) — NEW
Opportunity adjusted for lead cost: the cheaper the leads relative to deal fundamentals, the higher the ROI score. This directly weighs cost-to-market against reward — a great market with sky-high lead costs (e.g. Miami) scores lower than a solid market with cheap leads.
✨ Hidden Gem Flag
A market is flagged a Hidden Gem when it is small (under ~150k), low competition (≤3 of 10), below-average lead cost, and still scores 55+ on opportunity — the cheap, easy markets bigger players overlook.
